Chinchilla Garden Houses

There was an article about the chinchilla garden houses you can see below (more photos to come) in the German magazin “Ein Herz für Tiere” in August 2002. Sorry ... Detailed information about the chin houses can be found in “Das große Chinchilla-Handbuch”, a German book for owners of pet chinchilla, only.  For those of you who are able to speak German: The book is available at www.amazon.de
