Welcome  to The Chinchillaburg (Chinchilla Castle)!

These pages are exspecially for those who are already owner of a chinchilla. The book „Das Chinchilla- Handbuch" contains a great deal of basic knowledge about pet chinchillas for all of you who are able to speak German.

The wild chinchilla is usually found in attitudes between 2500 m* to 3700 m, but they are able to push forward into an area about 6100 m high. Because they are threatened with extinction it is difficult to see them in their natural surroundings. The last population of the “chinchilla lanigera” lives in Chile. The Giant Chinchilla is extinct due to excessive hunting and habitat destruction.
It is the chase after money which causes a decrease in the number of population. Remember that there are many kinds of animals which are hunted because of their special fur. Please do not support this kind of business.
